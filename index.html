Understanding IV Therapy

Intravenous therapy, often referred to as IV therapy, is a medical treatment that involves the direct administration of essential nutrients, vitamins, minerals, and fluids into the bloodstream through a sterile needle and intravenous line. This method bypasses the digestive system, ensuring that the body absorbs nutrients more efficiently. IV therapy has been used in clinical settings for decades to address various medical conditions, but in recent years, it has gained popularity as a wellness and rejuvenation treatment.
